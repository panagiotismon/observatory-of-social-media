Trends and Trustworthiness

This tool appears the most shared posts. You can check also their votes in an interactive way according to the level of trustworthiness you want to take into account.


{{ div4|safe }}
The most shared posts are depicted according to the number of shares and the posts' date. Hover the mouse over the bars to see the total number of votes and the text of the post as well. The green color represents a post with more trustful votes as the red color represents post with more mistrusted votes. The blue color indicates that the post has equal trusted and mistrusted votes and the grey color indicates that the post has no vote (or ignored).
